1. 流利度是最重要的。流利度由连接词体现。

   进阶连接词：because表强因果，比较正式，我们可以用simply because,mainly because,basically\
   表示转折：even though,still\
   but still, having said that ,that said\
   I reckon(I think) , I assume, I suppose\
   for example -like,let's say\
   so- and that's why 
2.   词汇多样性\
   very - quite,incredibly (I eat out quite often)\
   convenient - hassle-free 省麻烦的\
   reloable / favorite -go-to\
   difficult - struggle (struggle with homework)\
   choose - go for\
   prefer -would rahter do\
   happy - over moon \
   important -necessary\
   be good at -have a real knack for\
   go to/visit -hit\
   play with + 玩具 -hang out with my close buddies =spend quality time with\
   scroll on my phone/apps/social media
3. 不要一直用I开头\
   可以用Doing，which去开头\
   当描述照片的时候，应该是用现在时
